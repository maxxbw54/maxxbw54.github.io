Bio

I am a Research Scientist (Post-Doc) in the School of Computing and Information Systems (SCIS) at Singapore Management University (SMU). I got my PhD in August 2021 from SCIS at SMU, supervised by Prof. David Lo. Before I joined SMU, I received my Master Degree of Engineering in June 2017 from the Colloge of Computer Science and Technology of Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Shanping Li and Prof. Xin Xia. During my master career, I was a visiting student in the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, supervised by Prof. Zhenchang Xing.
